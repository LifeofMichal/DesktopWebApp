About the site

This portfolio of mine is basically a testing ground in web-development. Using an automated, semi-randomized picture display "algorithm" made in Javascript and JQuery I want to show You, what I have worked with, and working on now. The main purpose of this is simplicity and getting You interested by browsing around and makinf thoughts of your own based on just images; "Pictures say more than 1000 words", right? Randomization feature also shows You new things each time (I hope)
Since the point is to communicate through pictures not words, I dropped needless labels. Look closely, pay attention and I'm sure you will figure everything out! ;)

An option to comment will be added in the future, thus utilizing the viewers to label my work; an artistic experiment of sorts. For now, enjoy, browse around, and if you want to know more, feel free to get in touch ;)
